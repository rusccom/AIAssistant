Variants Template | Separate Products Template

Checked: Group rows with same Product Title into variants (iPhone 15 -> 2 variants)
Unchecked: Create separate product for each row (iPhone 15 128GB + iPhone 15 256GB = 2 products)

CSV Format: Product Title, Product Description, Variant Title, Price, SKU, Status
Auto-detects: Comma (,) or Semicolon (;) separators
Important: Save as CSV UTF-8 from Excel

Group by variants mode (recommended):
iPhone 15;Smartphone Apple;128GB Black;89999;IP15-128-BK;active
iPhone 15;Smartphone Apple;256GB Black;104999;IP15-256-BK;active
-> Creates 1 product "iPhone 15" with 2 variants

Each row = separate product mode:
iPhone 15 128GB;Smartphone Apple Black;Default Title;89999;IP15-128-BK;active
iPhone 15 256GB;Smartphone Apple Black;Default Title;104999;IP15-256-BK;active
-> Creates 2 separate products

Price: In kopecks (89999 = 899.99) or rubles (899.99 auto-converted)
JSON Format: Array with title, description, variants fields
